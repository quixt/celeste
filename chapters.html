Chapter 1: Forsaken City

Chapter One is set in an old, abandoned city. The city is covered in snow and is in a derelict state. This is an entry level and displays many mechanics to be used later in the game.

Introduces:

StrawberryWinged StrawberryJump Crystals

Chapter 2: Old Site

Chapter Two is set in an old, creepy dungeon-like subterranen expanse. In this level you meet the other-self character, who is a representation of the players doubtful side.

Introduces:

Star BlocksDisappearing PlatformsSkull Binoculars

Chapter 3: Celestial Resort

Chapter Three is set in a hotel on Mount Celeste called the Celestial Resort. It has been out of business for years but is attended by a man named Mr. Oshiro, who struggles with his own personal demons.

Introduces:

Keys & LocksSinking PlatformsSludge
