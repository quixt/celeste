CLIMB Chapters Characters Mechanics

Madeline

Madeline is a young woman who is determined to climb the mountain known as Celeste. She is brave and determined, and she has a heart of gold. She is also very determined and stubborn, which sometimes gets her into trouble.

Other Self

Madeline's other self is a dark, shadowy version of herself that represents her negative emotions and self-doubt. This character is always trying to hold Madeline back and make her doubt herself. Madeline has to face her other self in order to overcome her fears and doubts and become her true self.

Theo

Theo is a kind and gentle adventurer who helps the protagonist, Madeline, on her journey to the top of Celeste Mountain. Theo provides emotional support and encourages Madeline when she is struggling. He is always there for her, even when she is at her lowest.

Mr. Oshiro

Oshiro is the owner and manager of the Celestial hotel. He's a kind and gentle man, always looking out for his guests and employees. He's been running the hotel for years, and it's become like a second home to him. He's always happy to help out however he can, and he's always willing to lend a listening ear.
